**Al HissN (الحصن) - Your Digital Protector**

Al HissN is a next-generation, privacy-first security application designed to shield you from digital threats completely on-device. Utilizing a modern, sleek interface and cutting-edge local AI models, Al HissN ensures that your personal data never leaves your smartphone. 

**Key Features:**
• **On-Device AI Protection:** Analyzes suspicious links, text, and apps completely offline. No cloud APIs, no data leaks.
• **Local VPN Shield:** Blocks known malicious domains, trackers, and phishing sites without routing your traffic through external servers.
• **Secure Keyboard (IME):** Protects your keystrokes from keyloggers with a hardened, local-only keyboard.
• **Accessibility Scanner:** Proactively warns you against overlay attacks, deceptive UI, and malicious screen readers.
• **Zero Data Collection:** We don't collect, share, or sell your data. Your privacy is mathematically guaranteed.
